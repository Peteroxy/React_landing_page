What Brought About The Project?

The creation of this recipe app was done to help solve the problem many men have. Men have been prone to having health issues in their young age as a result of the inability to eat health foods. When they are asked the reson for not eating healthy, they give many reasons such as; "Not knowing what recipe to use in cooking", "Not feeling like cooking", "Not being able to know how to cook", "Not being able to order The right Recipe" and lots more.

To help solve this issue, i decided to create an app that can connect via an API to get nice dishes that are cookable. The recipe app also contains the ingredients that would be required and the procedures to be taken.

It should be noted that this app is still in its infancy and many modifications are intended. Some of such modifications include, meal planners with dates, a button to arder ingredients after collaboration with farmers and marketers, a heart icon to save favourite meals, and a login page to prevent others from seeing your meals, meal according to nationality, lots more.
